The Problem

Ben's Current Reality

Every morning, six data dumps land from Spectrix, Tessatura, TicketSolve and others. Each one formatted differently — inconsistent column headers, American date formats, random gaps. Ben pulls them into Excel macros he built over years. It works, but only because Ben built it.

  • Single point of failure. If Ben isn't in, it either waits or someone has a nervous breakdown trying to replicate his process.
  • The Harry Potter moment. A 12MB file from New York dropped with zero warning. Staffing chaos. No forecast, no heads-up — just a wall of data and a scramble.
  • Power BI that gathered dust. A dashboard was built. Nobody used it. The team needed answers, not another tool to learn.
  • Peter can't self-serve. Every strategic question routes through Ben. If the answer isn't in front of him already, it waits.
The Solution

What Milo Does

Milo lets anyone at Secure My Booking ask questions about your data in plain English and get answers with explanations — the why, not just the what.

No SQL. No pivot tables. No waiting for Ben. Just type a question and get an answer that makes sense.

Critically: Milo works with messy spreadsheet data. You don't need a data warehouse. Milo connects to what you've already got — the CSVs, the exports, the formats you're already working with.
